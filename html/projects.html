Jawa Clinic

This project is a clinic appointment system designed to streamline appointment bookings, manage clinic staff, and allow patients, doctors, receptionists, and managers to interact efficiently. The system implements role-based APIs for four user categories: patients, doctors, receptionists, and managers. The system supports a multi-step booking cycle for appointments, allowing patients to book appointments, view visit details, and enabling doctors to document diagnoses and services provided. It also provides features for managing clinic staff, services, and pricing.

AccuBook (Bookie)

This platform is designed for small to medium-sized stores, shops, and traders who rely on traditional debt books or social media pages to manage their business. It combines debt management with store management, providing an all-in-one solution. Users can digitally record and track withdrawals and deposits with customers and suppliers, generate detailed financial reports, and manage their finances more efficiently. Additionally, the platform includes a product display feature, allowing traders to upload product images, add descriptions and prices, and share the link directly with customers via messaging apps like WhatsApp.

Coffee Shop Website (Figma)

This project is a fully designed Coffee Shop Website created using Figma, showcasing a seamless user experience through multiple pages, each developed in two key design phases: wireframing and mockups. The website features several primary pages, including the Home Page, which introduces the shop with featured products and special offers, and the Contact Us Page, which provides essential location and contact details. The About Us Page highlights the story and values behind the shop, while the Payment Page ensures a secure and smooth checkout process. The Cart Page allows customers to review and adjust their orders, and the Product Customize Page lets users personalize their coffee orders to perfection. Lastly, the Menu Page displays the full range of coffee, desserts, and more, with detailed descriptions and prices. This project demonstrates a clear focus on customer-friendly design, ensuring a user-centric approach throughout the entire experience. .
